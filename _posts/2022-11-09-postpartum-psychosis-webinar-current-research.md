We hosted a webinar discussing current knowledge on postpartum psychosis, sponsored by the Section of Women’s Mental Health of the European Psychiatric Association and in collaboration with Action on Postpartum Psychosis (APP).

Professors Arianna Di Florio, Ian Jones and Dr Marisa Casanova Dias were joined by Dr Sally Wilson from APP to talk about research that is currently happening both in Cardiff and more globally.

Read more at the [blog post on National Centre for Mental Health's website](https://www.cardiff.ac.uk/news/view/2737065-postpartum-psychosis-what-has-the-research-told-us-so-far).
