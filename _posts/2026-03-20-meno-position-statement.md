Professor Arianna Di Florio attended the launch of the Royal College of Psychiatrists first ever position statement and mental health.

[Our research paper](https://www.nature.com/articles/s44220-024-00292-4) on first onsets of severe mental health conditions during the perimenopause provided key scientific evidence in the statement.

Read more at the [blog post on National Centre for Mental Health's website](https://www.cardiff.ac.uk/reproductive-mental-health-programme/resources).
