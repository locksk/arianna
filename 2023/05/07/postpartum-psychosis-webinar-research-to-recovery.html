Webinar on Postpartum Psychosis - From Research to Recovery

arianna di florio and jessica yang May 07, 2023 July 24, 2026
postpartum-psychosis bipolar-disorder research webinar

As part of Maternal Mental Health Awareness Week 2023, we hosted a webinar discussing current knowledge on postpartum psychosis.

Professors Arianna Di Florio, Ian Jones and researchers Jessica Yang, Dr Jo Hodgekins and Camilla Car were joined by Dr Sally Wilson and Laura Dockrill from APP to talk about the impact of postpartum psychosis and the research efforts currently going on in the UK.

Read more at the blog post on National Centre for Mental Health’s website.
